Now that you've gotten a chance to look at your organization, how about describing your organization for everyone else?

Go to the Early Talent Jam and find the forum called 'My Organization'. You should create a post that includes the following:

  • 1-2 sentence description of what YOUR role is.
  • 1-2 sentence description of what your organization does
  • Description of the people in your organization
    • How many people? Across how many geographic locations?
    • Note: Don't use names.
