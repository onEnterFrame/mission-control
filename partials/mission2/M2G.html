Communicating across cultures is always rewarding; but, can often be challenging as well. Though, the way we get better at it is to simply do it. So…

Go to the Early Talent Jam and look for the thread on 'your modes of transportation.'

Write a response to the following questions about your preferred modes of travel to:

  • Get to work.
  • Visit close family and friends.
  • Go out to a nice restaurant or club to see a concert.

Be as descriptive as possible. Your goal is to try to share some of the experience that you go through.

Example:

I live very close by the office where I work. I often walk; though, if the weather is especially poor, I will drive. It takes about the same time either way because of all the traffic signals between my home and my work.
