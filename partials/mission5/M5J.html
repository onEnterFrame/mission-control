It usually takes at least TWO to have a meaningful and productive conversation, right? The Talent Check-In is a meaningful conversation with your manager that happens throughout the year, not just at times traditionally reserved for interim and year-end reviews. There are a variety of different types of Talent Check-In's, but all are focused on ensuring that you have what you need to be successful. And, let's face it, you might need to speak to some of the local community members directly or answer questions.
